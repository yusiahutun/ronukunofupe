50 jaw-dropping celebrity birthday cakes that need to be seen to be believed

Feast your eyes on these incredible celebratory bakes…

    WATCH: 12 incredible celebrity birthday cakes you'll want to taste

    While we can all relate to David Beckham receiving a Colin the Caterpillar cake for his birthday, what we secretly dream of is something even half as spectacular as some of these celebrity birthday cakes.

    From decadent chocolate cakes to extravagant tiered masterpieces and adorable homemade creations, it's hard to pick a favourite from this amazing collection. Take a look at some of our favourite celebrity birthday cakes below…

    Indie Wicks

    Joe Wicks' three-year-old daughter was given a towering drip cake covered with rainbow buttercream icing and topped with flowers, butterflies and hearts for her birthday.

    Amongst the icing sugar roses and pink chocolate hearts was a giant pink crown, while a mini unicorn and number three sat on the base. If you ask us, it looks too good to eat!

    Princess Andre

    Peter Andre treated his 14-year-old daughter Princess to a diamond-encrusted birthday cake. Fans were left in awe of the showstopping bake, rushing to the comments to share their thoughts on the birthday girl's delicious treat. "Now this is right up my street with all that sparkle!" revealed a fan, whilst another agreed: "Definitely a cake that’s fit for a princess."

    Junior Andre

    Handmade by bespoke cake company Sophie Bakes Cakes, Junior's indulgent chocolate surprise was made from several layers of decadent chocolate sponge, decorated with chocolate treats and iced with a dark chocolate ganache. Did we mention it was chocolate?

    Romeo Beckham

    Victoria and David pulled out all the stops for Romeo Beckham's 19th birthday party. The star's showstopping surprise featured several layers of sponge topped with football-themed decorations, including a nod to David's football team Inter Miami. The middle tier read: "Happy Birthday Romeo" in large black icing, while sparklers topped each tier of the jaw-dropping treat.

    With honeycomb football decoration and lashings of thick icing, Romeo's multi-tiered cake was grand enough to be at a royal wedding.
