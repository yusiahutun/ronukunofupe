    What types of Skrill accounts are available? Which one is best suited for me?

    Skrill keeps its services very simple not to confuse customers. They offer two types of accounts:

    1. Personal account

    Excellent for people who wish to:

    2. Business account

    Skrill account registration procedure

    Many people usually have difficulties in using Skrill service because they don’t create accounts using the right process. To help you do so correctly, we have come up with a step by step guide that will highlight key points that should be followed.

  • Launch your browser: We recommend using Chrome if you are operating on a smartphone. Also, make sure you are connected to your Wi-Fi network, or your data connection is on.
  • Go to Skrill official website: Type in the address in your browser and hit the “Enter” button. Wait as the page loads. You will find yourself on the signup page. Note that creating an account is entirely free, just like PayPal.
  • Enter your email: Be sure to enter a valid email address as you will receive a verification mail. If you need a business one, you will see an option at the bottom of the page, click on it.
  • Enter your password: Choose a strong password of above 8 characters. Include symbols like #$%^&*! To make it impossible to crack. Also, don’t forget to mix capital and small letters with numbers. Never use a weak password as the account will hold your funds, if hacked you might lose your hard earned money.
  • Press the “Next” button: Wait as the next registration page is being loaded. The wait time will depend on how strong your internet connection is.
  • Provide your details: Specify all the required information. Do not lie, use the details in your national identity card or passport as you will be required to scan it for verification. Enter your accurate mobile number, date of birth, country and the city you live in.
  • Tap the “Continue to step 2” button: Again, you have to be patient as the next page loads.
  • Provide your valid address: This is the billing address that will be used to transact money in and out of your bank account.
  • Click on the “Next” button: This will take you to the final step of the registration.
  • Select your preferred currency: We recommend using US dollars or Euro since it is easy to convert any figures in the currencies to Kenyan Shillings.
  • Confirm whether you are not a robot: Complete the Google reCAPTCHA challenge to do so.
  • Opt into Skrill’s emails: This is optional, and you can unsubscribe anytime you want.
  • Click on the “Open Account” option: This button is usually located at the bottom of the page.
  • Verify your email address: Now, you have successfully created an account. However, you won’t be able to use it without verifying your email address, as we mentioned previously.

    How to connect and verify your debit/credit card on Skrill

    The first thing you will probably think of after opening is depositing to your wallet. You have to check your debit or credit card to make deposits without restriction. Unverified cards have FSA limits. Use the following steps to add and verify your cards. Note that any mastercard or visa card that can transact internationally is acceptable.

  • Login to your account.
  • Go to “Card or Bank accounts” tab, which is visible from your dashboard.
  • Tap “Add debit or credit card.”
  • Enter your card details: Number, CVV code and then save it.
  • Once saved, go to “Card or Bank accounts” again: You will see the credit/debit card you added.
  • Click on the “Verify” button just next to the card.
  • Type your CVV code again and click the “Save” option.
  • A new page will be loaded up telling you that Skrill will debit some amount of money from your account. You have to check and confirm this transaction.
  • Click on “Debit amount” so that Skrill deducts some money from your bank.

    How to remove any debit or credit card you added to Skrill

    At some point, you might want to remove your old bank details for privacy, security or personal issues. However, this is never possible since Skrill doesn’t let anyone delete “Primary” card information. This is what you can do in case you find yourself in this situation.

  • Get a valid and less important card. You can use online credit card generators to do so.
  • Login to your account.
  • Under “Cards and Bank Accounts” click on add a new card.
  • Feed the system with less important account details.
  • Just next to it, click on “Make as Primary.”
  • Now you can delete the previously “Primary” card information.
