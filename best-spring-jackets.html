20 Best Spring Jackets of 2024

The Cropped Trench Coat

Rue Sophie Enola Crop Trench Coat

The Cropped Trench Coat

Rue Sophie Enola Crop Trench Coat

The full-length trench coat’s shorter, boxier, and edgier younger sister, the cropped trench coat is set to be everywhere this season. From L.A.-based brand Rue Sophie, this pretty blush-toned style is a fun switch-up from more obvious camel-colored versions.

The Belted Leather Jacket

Ulla Johnson Aidan Belted Leather Jacket

The Belted Leather Jacket

Ulla Johnson Aidan Belted Leather Jacket

Few pieces are as essential to a wardrobe as a leather jacket, but if popular moto and oversize styles aren’t quite your speed, try this more delicate option. The tying belt will help cinch you in at the waist, while puffed sleeves add to the overall feminine vibe.

The Tailored Blazer

Veronica Beard Miller Dickey Jacket

The Tailored Blazer

Veronica Beard Miller Dickey Jacket

Fitted blazers are currently having a moment, alongside their oversize counterparts. This impeccably tailored option from Veronica Beard will become a cornerstone of your wardrobe. It’s versatile enough to complete both casual and more formal ensembles.

The Oversize Denim Jacket

Ganni Tint Oversize Denim Jacket

The Oversize Denim Jacket

Ganni Tint Oversize Denim Jacket

Ganni’s au courant take on the perennial denim jacket is roomy, for easy layering, and includes a practical extra front pocket. Even better, it’s made with eco-conscious organic and recycled cotton.

The Western Fringe Jacket

Found Suede Fringe Jacket

The Western Fringe Jacket

Found Suede Fringe Jacket

Blame it on Beyoncé or the Spring 2024 runways—the yee-haw agenda is once again in full swing. Lean into the western vibes with this soft fringed coat. All that’s left to do is pick up some festive cowboy boots.

The Padded Bomber Jacket

Loewe Padded Bomber Jacket in Nylon

The Padded Bomber Jacket

Loewe Padded Bomber Jacket in Nylon

Credit: Loewe

“Bomber jackets are also trending this season, and this Loewe version is on my wish list,” Comroe says. “The padded design breathes new life into the recognizable bomber silhouette, offering an edgy twist. Spotted on It girls like Taylor Russell and Kendall Jenner, it has become a must-have piece for casual springtime looks.”

The Tweed Jacket

Maje Belted-Waist Tweed Jacket

The Tweed Jacket

Maje Belted-Waist Tweed Jacket

Thanks to the rise of quiet luxury and “stealth wealth” dressing trends, tweed jackets are here to stay. Though Chanel deserves all the credit for originally popularizing the style, contemporary Parisian brand Maje also makes some of our favorite iterations. This long-line tweed coat from the brand’s latest spring collection would look just as effortless paired with the matching trousers as it would with some relaxed-fit jeans for contrast.

The Reimagined Biker Jacket

Khaite The Shallin Jacket

The Reimagined Biker Jacket

Khaite The Shallin Jacket

Credit: Khaite

Comroe notes biker jackets are back, too, but in more streamlined versions, without any of the signature chunky hardware. Case in point: this version from Khaite, complete with “gold accents that add a touch of edge without going too biker,” she says.

“This can be worn in a million different ways,” she adds. “Pair it with jeans and a white tee for a laid-back ensemble, or elevate the look with sleek black trousers and a chic sweater.”

The Feminine Cardigan

Nili Lotan Women’s Perah Wool Tipped Jacket

The Feminine Cardigan

Nili Lotan Women’s Perah Wool Tipped Jacket

Another quiet luxury pick, this Nili Lotan cardigan has intricate crocheting and elegant color-blocked details. Depending on the occasion, we recommend styling it with wide-leg jeans or an equally preppy tennis skirt.

The Quilted Utility Jacket

Barbour Annandale Quilted Shell Jacket

The Quilted Utility Jacket

Barbour Annandale Quilted Shell Jacket

“Blending practicality with a touch of sophistication, brands like Prada reimagined the utility jacket for Spring 2024 with luxurious touches, such as leather collars,” says Comroe. For a lightweight option, try this style from legacy (and royal-loved) brand Barbour, done with soft quilted details and corduroy trimmings.

The Full-Length Trench Coat

Ralph Lauren Double-Breasted Twill Trench Coat

The Full-Length Trench Coat

Ralph Lauren Double-Breasted Twill Trench Coat

That said, there’s nothing wrong with sticking to a classic. Ralph Lauren’s full-length trench coat fits the bill, featuring a double-breasted silhouette, shoulder epaulets, and storm flaps for protection. The perfect transitional staple, it is also made with crisp cotton and unlined for comfort.

The Structured Linen Jacket

Reformation Larsen Linen Jacket

The Structured Linen Jacket

Reformation Larsen Linen Jacket

For warmer spring days that require an ultra-lightweight layer, this 100 percent linen coat is a must. Not only is it ideal for rising temperatures, it also still looks polished, due to its padded, waist-defining shape.

The Avant-Garde Scarf Jacket

Source Unknown Edmea Scarf-Detailed Jacket

The Avant-Garde Scarf Jacket

Source Unknown Edmea Scarf-Detailed Jacket

If your idea of ultimate comfort is being swaddled in a throw blanket, allow us to introduce you to this elevated, wearable alternative. Equal parts cozy and sophisticated, this drapey, scarf-detailed style is crafted with a mid-weight mélange wool for moderate warmth and a dose of textural appeal.

The Sleeveless Trench Coat

COS Sleeveless Linen-Blend Trench Coat

The Sleeveless Trench Coat

COS Sleeveless Linen-Blend Trench Coat

You’ve probably gathered by now that trench coats—in all forms—continue to be a major spring tenant. Another variation to add to your list: the sleeveless trench, ideal for anyone who prefers a little more breathing room up top. This breezy linen version from COS is great to throw on over a button-up to add an element of dimension.

The Everyday Barrel Jacket

Everlane The Denim Barrel Jacket

The Everyday Barrel Jacket

Everlane The Denim Barrel Jacket

Now 20% Off

As much as we love blue denim, this crisp cocoa-colored jean jacket is a nice way to break the mold. With its collarless, structured fit, it’s just as simple to style with your favorite basics, yet feels a smidge more refined.

The Poncho-Style Jacket

Zara Hooded Jacket

The Poncho-Style Jacket

Zara Hooded Jacket

On gloomy days, you’ll be glad to have this practical yet stylish option in your arsenal. This water-resistant, poncho-style coat has a hood, elastic cuffs, a drawstring cord at the hem, and even a hidden zipper to help keep out the elements.

The Striped Blazer

Jacquemus Grosgrain-Trimmed Striped Woven Blazer

The Striped Blazer

Jacquemus Grosgrain-Trimmed Striped Woven Blazer

Designers have dictated that graphic stripes are back in a big way this season. The vertical beige lines on this fitted number will work well to elongate your torso. Other standout details include a grosgrain ribbon belt that threads through a cutout in the rear.

The Packable Hero

FP Movement Patricia Packable Poncho Puffer

The Packable Hero

FP Movement Patricia Packable Poncho Puffer

For those unexpected cold snaps or temperate afternoons that turn into chilly evenings, this packable puffer will be your savior. Yes, it’s sporty, but it’s water-repellent and made with a down alternative designed for lightweight warmth and peak compressibility. In fact, it even folds down into its own pocket, making it the ultimate travel accessory.

The Statement Cashmere Coat

The Row Priske Cashmere Coat

The Statement Cashmere Coat

The Row Priske Cashmere Coat

Every closet could use a good statement coat. The Row’s ankle-grazing red cashmere style offers just the right of amount of ’80s flair with its figure-flattering cut and subtle shoulder pads. Throw this on over a slinky date-night dress, or pair it with some coordinating red leather gloves for a truly head-turning look.

The Beige Cargo Jacket

Aligne Melon Utility Jacket

The Beige Cargo Jacket

Aligne Melon Utility Jacket

Comroe also notes that utility coats in muted beige hues were seen all over Spring 2024 collections. This cropped cargo style aligns perfectly with the trend and offers some unique accents, including hidden button fastenings and offset button details on the waistband.
