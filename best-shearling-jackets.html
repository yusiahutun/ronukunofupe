Best Oversized Shearling Coat

Khaite Shellar Oversized Leather & Lambskin Shearling Jacket

7

Best Oversized Shearling Coat

Khaite Shellar Oversized Leather & Lambskin Shearling Jacket

Khaite's Shellar leather and lambskin shearling jacket has that retro look that feels reminiscent to the shearling-collar coat Marlon Brando famously wore in On the Waterfront. But the balloon sleeves and oversize fit give it the feminine modern flair Catherine Holstein is known for. Actress Laura Harrier gets it: She took an Instagram selfie wearing the coat in black with the caption "cozy."

Size Range: S to L

8

Best Street Style Shearling Coat

Saks Potts Black Foxy Leather Coat

8

Best Street Style Shearling Coat

Saks Potts Black Foxy Leather Coat

Saks Potts is to blame for bringing back the Penny Lane coat craze. The brand's signature foxy leather coat propelled the brand into the mainstream a couple years ago, but it's one of the only majorly trendy street style pieces that has stood the test of time. Regardless of its It-girl status, the coat is more than just a moment: Its shearling cuff and collar details plus waist-cinching pink buckle belt make it an instant classic.

Size Range: S to L

Best Affordable Shearling Coat

Topshop Chunky Faux Shearling Coat

Topshop's extra-fluffy faux-shearling coat is a Nordstrom shopper favorite. Reviewers praise the chunky coat for its accessible price point and luxe look. One writes, "It's warm and not boxy and a great price for a long faux-shearling coat. I wear size Small and the 6 fit best—I'm 5'10" and the length is good. Get it while you can!"

10

Best Investment Shearling Coat

Miu Miu Nappa Leather Jacket with Shearling Collar

Credit: Courtesy of Miu Miu

Miu Miu's Nappa Leather Jacket has a removable shearling collar, which means you can easily wear this piece year-round on balmy summer nights and warmer winter days. And if you're not convinced of its style prowess, look no further than Emma Corrin in the fall campaign. She wore the jacket styled in the most off-kilter Miu Miu way: with little leather shorts held up by not one but two belts, and a pair of the Miu Miu multi-buckle moto boots social media can't stop obsessing over.

Size Range: FR 36 to FR 50

Best Cropped Shearling Coat

11

Best Cropped Shearling Coat

Acne Studios Brown Cropped Shearling Jacket

Now 47% Off

Acne Studio's leather shearling buckle jacket has become one of the brand's best-selling items, and is produced season after season. But if you want something just as warm that feels a little different, this cropped shearling jacket by the brand is it. The bright orange cuff and collar add an unexpected color pop to the silhouette, and the self-tie closure makes it easy to wear undone to show off your under-coat styling.

Size Range: FR 32 to FR 42
