Jennifer Lopez and Alex Rodriguez's Relationship Timeline from First Date to Engagement

Since Jennifer Lopez and Alex Rodriguez started dating, they've quickly become one of Hollywood's most adorable power couples—but now J-Rod is no more. Here, we take a look back at all of the key moments in their relationship.

May 2005

Jennifer Lopez and then-husband Marc Anthony attend a baseball game at Shea Stadium in Queens, New York, in May 2005 where they meet New York Yankee, Alex Rodriguez. Of their very first meeting, Lopez told Sports Illustrated in June 2019, "We shook hands, and it was this weird electricity for, like, three seconds... Three to five seconds of looking at somebody right in their eyes, and getting stuck."

Mike Ehrmann//Getty Images

Jennifer Lopez and Marc Anthony with New York Yankee Alex Rodriguez on May 21, 2005.

Per Today, Anthony and Lopez got married in 2004, separated in 2011, and finalized their divorce in 2014. They have two children together, Max and Emme. Meanwhile, Rodriguez was married to Cynthia Scurtis from 2002 to 2008 per Daily Mail, and they have two daughters together, Natasha and Ella.

Rodriguez later revealed that he'd actually gotten a signed photo of Lopez in the late '90s, which he's kept ever since. Basically, their relationship was written in the stars from the start.

February 2017

Lopez and Rodriguez reconnect when they bump into each other at a restaurant.

In April 2017, Lopez tells Ellen DeGeneres (per Entertainment Tonight), "I was having lunch somewhere and I saw him as he passed by. Afterwards, I went outside, and for some reason I felt like tapping him on the shoulder." Rodriguez made the next move: "He texted me, said, 'Let's go out to dinner,' and I said, 'OK.' We had a nice dinner."

In their joint interview with Vanity Fair, released in October 2017, Lopez reveals, "I had just come from a promo for my show, Shades of Blue [in which she plays N.Y.P.D. detective Harlee Santos], so I’m dressed like my character, like a boy—Timberlands, jeans, curly short hair. He looks at me. I say, 'It’s Jennifer.' He says, 'You look so beautiful.'" What a charmer.

In the same interview, Rodriguez sweetly reveals that he had first date nerves saying, "I didn’t know if it was a date. Maybe we were seeing each other at night because of her work schedule. I went in uneasy, not knowing her situation." He also explains why they hit it off so well: "We’re both Leos, we’re both from New York, we’re both Latino... and about 20 other things."

March 2019

Rodriguez and Lopez get engaged while on vacation in the Bahamas.

Following their engagement announcement, a source tells People, "Jennifer is ecstatic and so are their families and friends. Very few people knew Alex planned on proposing. It was very much a surprise for Jennifer. It was just how Alex wanted it."

Even the Obamas congratulate the couple on their engagement.

May 2019

The couple walk the pink carpet at the 2019 Met Gala together, and Lopez's low-cut Versace gown is simply jaw-dropping.

Theo Wargo//Getty Images

The same month, Lopez tells Entertainment Tonight, "I think we have a pretty busy year ahead of us, so you know, we are [taking it slow]... There's no rush. We really see this as something that's gonna be forever, and we're gonna just take our time and do it right. And do it not rushed."

July 2019

Lopez celebrates her 50th birthday on July 23, 2019, while Rodriguez turns 44 on July 27. Both share romantic Instagram posts to celebrate the occasions, and J.Lo surprises A-Rod on stage at one of her concerts with a birthday cake.

September 2019

Even though they're taking their wedding planning slowly, Lopez and Rodriguez share photos from their engagement party on social media.

During an interview with EXTRA TV, Lopez revealed that her twins would be "thrilled" if she had a baby with Rodriguez. Per Lopez, "I think they would love to have a brother or sister." She continues, "We really form a beautiful blended family and I think they would all be thrilled."

November 2019

Of her relationship with Rodriguez, Lopez tells GQ:

"We're really alike in our drive, in our ambition. He's an athlete. He wanted to be the best. I'm the same way. I just want to be great at what I do. We're both like that. We're both super-hard workers. We're driven by our passions. And we have a tremendous amount of respect for each other. Everybody in the room can be talking, and if he says something, I key in on it, and if everybody in the room is kinda in his ear and I go, 'I don't think that's the right thing,' he'll hear me; he'll listen. He knows I only have his best interest at heart, and I only want him to thrive and succeed and to be his best self. And I believe that he wants that for me. He wants me to be seen and to get what I deserve. Like, he's made his half a billion. And he's like, 'I want you to have that.'"

December 2019

A-Rod makes a hilarious cameo during J.Lo's Saturday Night Live appearance in December 2019. During the skit, the singer falls in love with Pete Davidson's character Chad, and Rodriguez is not best pleased.

January 2020

On January 2, Rodriguez and Lopez hit the red carpet for the Palm Springs International Film Festival. The singing superstar wore a mullet dress by British designer Richard Quinn.

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

September 2020

In an interview with People magazine, Lopez shared that having more time at home with her fiancé and their children has been the ultimate "blessing" throughout quarantining amid the coronavirus pandemic.

"Because Alex and I have both separately and now together lived a gypsy-type life, as both of our careers require tons of travel. The blessing of the quarantine for us was having dinner with the kids every night, for months and months," Lopez told the outlet. "That's been a huge blessing for us during this time."

The couple have shared multiple social media posts, especially on holidays, of their two families coming together for quality time.

March 2021

J.Lo and A-Rod reportedly broke up and called off their two-year engagement, according to outlets including Page Six and TMZ. Multiple sources also confirmed the news to People, with one insider saying, "This has been a long time coming." However, the couple denied the news in a joint statement, saying, "We are working through some things." They later showed some PDA in the Dominican Republic, where Lopez is filming her new movie, Shotgun Wedding.

April 2021

Lopez and Rodriguez officially break up and call off their engagement, announcing their split in a joint statement to the Today show.

We have realized we are better as friends and look forward to remaining so. We will continue to work together and support each other on our shared businesses and projects. We wish the best for each other and one another’s children. Out of respect for them, the only other comment we have to say is thank you to everyone who has sent kind words and support.
